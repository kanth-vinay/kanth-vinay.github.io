Mathematics Readiness Check

Grade 7 Missouri MAP-Aligned Activity

Prepared by Sharanya Narra, Lead Educator, Middle School Mathematics.

An original Grade 7 mathematics review activity created by ActiveSTEM and aligned to Missouri Grade 7 mathematics expectations.

Part 1: Core Skills Check

1.Rational Numbers

A submarine is operating at \(-24.5\) meters relative to sea level. It then ascends \(11.25\) meters. What is the submarine's new elevation?

2.Unit Rates

A local market sells a \(4.5\)-pound bag of apples for \($14.40\). What is the unit price per pound?

3.Percents

A family's restaurant bill is \($64.00\) before a \(7\%\) sales tax is applied. What is the total amount of the bill, including tax?

4.Expressions

Which expression is equivalent to \(3.5y - 2 + 1.5y + 6\)?

5.Equations

What value of \(x\) makes the equation \(4x - 7 = 25\) true?

6.Geometry

An interior designer sketches a circular rug for a lobby, with a diameter of \(10\) feet, as shown in the diagram below.

Which of the following is closest to the area of the rug? (Use \(\pi \approx 3.14\))

7.Scale Models

On a map, \(1\) centimeter represents \(15\) kilometers. If the distance between two cities on the map is \(4.5\) centimeters, what is the actual distance between them?

8.Proportionality

Which set of values represents a proportional relationship between \(x\) and \(y\)?

9.Probability

A fair spinner is divided into \(8\) equal sections, numbered \(1\) through \(8\). What is the probability that the spinner lands on a number less than 4?

10.Data Interpretation

Students measured the heights of bean plants. The median height was \(14\) inches, but the mean height was \(18\) inches. Which statement best explains this difference?

Part 2: Applied Reasoning Check

11.Percent Adjustments

A clothing store buys a jacket wholesale for \($40\). The store marks up the price by \(50\%\) to set the regular retail price. Later, the jacket is placed on sale for \(20\%\) off the regular retail price. What is the final sale price of the jacket?

12.Equivalent Expressions

Which expression is equivalent to \(-3(2k - 4) + 4k\)?

13.Coordinate Plane

Three vertices of a rectangle are plotted on the coordinate plane below.

What are the coordinates of the fourth vertex needed to complete the rectangle?

14.Inequalities

A bicycle rental shop charges a \($15\) base fee plus \($4\) per hour. Sarah has at most \($40\) to spend. Which inequality correctly represents the number of hours, \(h\), Sarah can rent the bicycle?

15.Compound Probability

A bag contains \(5\) red marbles, \(3\) blue marbles, and \(2\) green marbles. A student draws one marble, replaces it in the bag, and then draws a second marble. What is the probability that the student draws a red marble followed by a blue marble?

16.Volume

A right rectangular prism is packed with smaller cubes that have side lengths of \(\frac{1}{2}\) inch. The main prism measures \(2\) inches by \(3\) inches by \(4\) inches. How many of the \(\frac{1}{2}\)-inch cubes does it take to completely fill the prism?

17.Expressions (Multi-Select)

Which of the following expressions are equivalent to \(-8x + 12\)? Select all that apply.

18.Short Response

A recipe for cookies requires \(\frac{3}{4}\) cup of sugar for every \(2\) cups of flour. If a baker uses \(6\) cups of flour, how many cups of sugar are needed?

Enter your answer as a decimal.

Part 3: Performance Tasks

19.Written Reasoning 1

Analyze a student's mathematical work for solving the following equation:

Step 1: 3(x - 4) = 15
Step 2: 3x - 4 = 15
Step 3: 3x = 19
Step 4: x = 19/3

Identify the specific error the student made in their reasoning. Explain the mathematically correct procedure, and state the correct value of x.

20.Written Reasoning 2

A streaming service offers two membership plans. Plan A costs \($12\) per month with a one-time \($15\) startup fee. Plan B costs \($15\) per month with absolutely no startup fee.

Does Plan A represent a proportional relationship between the number of months and the total cost? Explain your reasoning. Use the concept of a constant unit rate or the graph's y-intercept to justify your answer.

You will not be able to change your answers after submission.
