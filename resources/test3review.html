Answer & Hint

Answer: Relative minimum at (6,3).

Hint:

Question 12: Concavity

(Click to reveal answer)

Answer & Hint

Answer:

  • Inflection Point: (2,-11)
  • Concave Down: (-∞,2)
  • Concave Up: (2,∞)

Hint:

  1. Find the second derivative.
    f′(x)=3x2-12x
    f″(x)=6x-12
  2. Set f″(x)=0 to find potential inflection points:
