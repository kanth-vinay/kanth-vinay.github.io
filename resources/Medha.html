Directions

This is a targeted Unit 10 AP Calculus BC prep set, not a full-length exam. It is designed for a student who is struggling with series and needs fast diagnostic feedback before the AP test.

Do the no-calculator items without a calculator. Use a graphing calculator only where labeled. Responses save locally, and final submission locks answers and displays worked feedback.

Expression entries accept ^, pi, sqrt(x), ln(x), sin(x), cos(x), and standard arithmetic.
