Directions

This is a targeted gap practice — not a full-length exam. It covers topics identified as absent from the companion full-length test.

Section I contains 25 multiple-choice items. Section II contains 3 free-response-style problems with auto-scorable subparts. Calculator status is shown on each item.

Responses and FRQ work notes are saved locally in this browser. Final submission locks all responses and displays diagnostic feedback. Scores are approximate and for practice only; this is not an official College Board exam or an official AP score prediction.

Expression entries accept common notation such as ^, pi, sin(x), ln(x), sqrt(x), and radians.

FRQ auto-scoring is approximate. Use the worked solutions and, when possible, verify expression-heavy responses with a teacher or tutor. The show-your-work boxes are not auto-scored, but they should be used the way handwritten FRQ work is used on the AP exam.
