The Problem: Cognitive Overload

Students are juggling too many formats:

  • Instructor slides (PowerPoint/Keynote)
  • Static PDFs and worksheets
  • LMS modules (Canvas/Blackboard)
  • External video links and interactive apps

The UDL Solution: "Slides on Steroids" consolidates these into a single, interactive HTML learning hub, reducing cognitive load and lowering the barrier to entry for all learners.

Difference Quotient — Color-Coded Operations

Tip: press → to reveal steps.

Let \(f(x)=x^2\). Compute \(\dfrac{f(x+\class{op-add}{h})-f(x)}{\class{op-div}{h}}\).
\(\dfrac{(x\,\class{op-add}{+\,h})^2 - \class{cancel}{x^2}}{\class{op-div}{h}}\)
\(\dfrac{x^2\,\class{cancel}{+\,2xh + h^2} - \class{cancel}{x^2}}{\class{op-div}{h}}\)   (expand)
\(\dfrac{\class{cancel}{x^2} \;\class{op-sub}{-}\; \class{cancel}{x^2} \;\oplus\; 2x\,\class{op-mul}{\cdot}\,h \;\oplus\; h^2}{\class{op-div}{h}}\)
\(\dfrac{\class{op-mul}{2xh} + h^2}{\class{op-div}{h}} = \class{op-mul}{2x} + h\)
As \(h\to0\), the difference quotient \(\to 2x\).

Access, Equity & Support

Universal Access: Every interactive element has a static, printable equivalent (Press P for PDF). Keyboard and screen-reading are natively supported.

Campus Resources: Tutoring schedules, Disability Access Services, and counseling info are linked directly in Canvas and mirrored here to reduce friction.

Representation in Data: We explicitly discuss sampling bias in class datasets (e.g., who was excluded from the clinical trial?). Datasets rotate across diverse contexts so more students see their experiences reflected.

Making assumptions explicit and visible ensures all learners feel seen and supported.

Humanizing Teaching through Adaptation

  • Real‑time adaptation: I watch the in‑deck polls. If Not yet > 40%, we slow down and add a worked example; if Comfortable > 60%, we jump to applications.
  • Fostering Connection: The "Student Selector" and embedded whiteboards ensure every voice is heard, creating a truly collaborative environment.
  • Focusing on the Human Element: By automating the delivery of interactive content (dice, polls, embeds), I can step away from the podium and engage directly with groups.

AI should reduce clerical load so we can focus on human connection and responsiveness.
