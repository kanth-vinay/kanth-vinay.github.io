Notation Guide for this Round:

Let $\lfloor x \rfloor$ be the largest integer that is $\leq x$.
For example: $\lfloor \pi \rfloor = 3$, $\lfloor 8.9 \rfloor = 8$, and $\lfloor 5 \rfloor = 5$.
