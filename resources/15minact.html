a. Find the instantaneous velocity after \(3\) seconds.

c. At what time does the probe hit the ground?

d. With what velocity does the probe hit the ground?

2 pointsCorrect general velocity \(v(a)\).
2 pointsCorrect ground-hit time and impact velocity.
