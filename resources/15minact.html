Vector Properties & Operations (You may use this reference sheet throughout the challenge)

Position \& Magnitude

  • Component Form:
    From \(P(x_1, y_1)\) to \(Q(x_2, y_2)\):
    \(\mathbf{v} = \langle x_2 - x_1, y_2 - y_1 \rangle\)
  • Magnitude:
    \(|\mathbf{v}| = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2}\)

Vector Algebra

  • Scalar Multiplication:
    \(k\langle a, b \rangle = \langle ka, kb \rangle\)
  • Vector Addition:
    \(\langle a, b \rangle + \langle c, d \rangle = \langle a+c, b+d \rangle\)
  • Unit Vector:
    \(\mathbf{u} = \frac{\mathbf{v}}{|\mathbf{v}|}\)

Graphical Methods

  • Tip-to-Tail: Place the terminal point of the first vector at the initial point of the second vector. The resultant runs from the origin to the terminal point of the second vector.
  • Parallelogram: Vectors must share the exact same initial point.
