Big Idea 1: Descriptive Stats

The Story of Data

Before we analyze, we must see.

  • What is the shape? (Bell curve? Skewed?)
  • Where is the center? (Mean vs Median)
  • Who are the outliers?

"Data doesn't speak. We speak for it."

Big Idea 2: Probability

Uncertainty $\to$ Mathematics

The Logic of Chance

Randomness is not chaos. It follows rules.

  • Normal Distribution (The Bell Curve)
  • Sampling Distributions
  • "How likely is this result?"

Big Idea 3: Inference

The Power of Conclusion

Using a small sample to judge a massive population.

Hypothesis Testing: The courtroom trial of science. guilty (reject) or not guilty (fail to reject)?

Sample $\to$ Population

RStudio Setup (Do it Right Now)

RStudio.cloud

  1. Log in to RStudio.cloud (or Posit Cloud).
  2. Click New Project in Top Right.
  3. Select "New RStudio Project".
  4. Wait for "Deploying..." (Takes ~30s).

Quarto Doc

  1. File → New File → Quarto Document.
  2. Title: "Day 1 Practice".
  3. Uncheck "Use Visual Editor" (Optional).
  4. Click Render to test PDF/HTML output.
