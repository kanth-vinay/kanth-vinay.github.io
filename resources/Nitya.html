Directions

This is an original practice section modeled on the current Enhanced ACT Mathematics test, focused entirely on Functions. It contains 45 multiple-choice questions, each with four answer choices.

This diagnostic uses the full Enhanced ACT Math timing: 50 minutes for 45 questions (about 67 seconds each). A calculator is permitted on every question. The questions are ordered to approximate the ACT structure, ramping from manageable multi-step problems to highly demanding algebraic and conceptual manipulation.

Responses are saved locally in this browser. Final submission locks all responses and displays diagnostic feedback with a scaled-score estimate.

Choose the best answer for each question. There is no penalty for guessing, so answer every question.
