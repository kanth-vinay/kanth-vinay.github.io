Directions

This is an original practice section modeled on the current Enhanced ACT Mathematics test, focused entirely on Trigonometry. It contains 41 multiple-choice questions, each with four answer choices.

This diagnostic uses the full Enhanced ACT Math timing: 50 minutes for 41 questions (about 73 seconds each). A calculator is permitted on every question. The questions are arranged from most accessible to most demanding — the final third requires multi-step reasoning built on familiar ideas, which is where the Enhanced format separates scores.

Unless a question states otherwise, angle measures are in degrees, and figures are provided for reference and may not be drawn to scale.

Responses are saved locally in this browser. Final submission locks all responses and displays diagnostic feedback with a scaled-score estimate. Scores are approximate and for practice only; this is not an official ACT exam or an official ACT score prediction.

Choose the best answer for each question. There is no penalty for guessing, so answer every question. Make sure your calculator is in the mode (degrees) the question intends.
