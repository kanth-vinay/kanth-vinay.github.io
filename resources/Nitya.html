Directions

This is an original practice section modeled on the current Enhanced ACT Mathematics test, focused entirely on Algebra. It contains 45 multiple-choice questions, each with four answer choices.

This diagnostic uses the full Enhanced ACT Math timing: 50 minutes for 45 questions (about 67 seconds each). A calculator is permitted on every question. The questions are arranged from most accessible to most demanding — the final third is deliberately ruthless, built on multi-step reasoning and trap distractors over ordinary algebra, which is exactly where the Enhanced format separates scores.

Responses are saved locally in this browser. Final submission locks all responses and displays diagnostic feedback with a scaled-score estimate. Scores are approximate and for practice only; this is not an official ACT exam or an official ACT score prediction.

Choose the best answer for each question. There is no penalty for guessing, so answer every question. Work each problem the way you would on the digital test: jump straight into computation when you can, and reserve your scratch reasoning for the multi-step items near the end.
