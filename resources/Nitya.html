Directions

This is an original practice section modeled on the current Enhanced ACT Mathematics test, focused entirely on Pre-Algebra and Number Skills. It contains 35 multiple-choice questions, each with four answer choices.

The full Enhanced ACT Math test gives 50 minutes for 45 questions. This single-topic diagnostic uses a proportional 45-minute window for its 35 questions. As on the real exam, a calculator is permitted on every question, and the questions are arranged from most accessible to most demanding — the final third requires multi-step reasoning built on familiar ideas, which is where the Enhanced format separates scores.

Responses are saved locally in this browser. Final submission locks all responses and displays diagnostic feedback with a scaled-score estimate. Scores are approximate and for practice only; this is not an official ACT exam or an official ACT score prediction.

Choose the best answer for each question. There is no penalty for guessing, so answer every question. Work each problem the way you would on the digital test: jump straight into computation when you can, and reserve your scratch reasoning for the multi-step items near the end.
