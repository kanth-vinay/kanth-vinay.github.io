Directions

This is an original practice section modeled on the current Enhanced ACT Mathematics test, focused entirely on Geometry. It contains 45 multiple-choice questions, each with four answer choices.

This diagnostic uses the full Enhanced ACT Math timing: 50 minutes for 45 questions (about 67 seconds each). A calculator is permitted on every question. The questions are arranged from most accessible to most demanding — the final third requires multi-step reasoning built on familiar ideas, which is where the Enhanced format separates scores.

Figures are provided for reference. Unless a question states otherwise, figures are not necessarily drawn to scale, all figures lie in a plane, and lines shown as straight are straight.

Responses are saved locally in this browser. Final submission locks all responses and displays diagnostic feedback with a scaled-score estimate. Scores are approximate and for practice only; this is not an official ACT exam or an official ACT score prediction.

Choose the best answer for each question. There is no penalty for guessing, so answer every question.
