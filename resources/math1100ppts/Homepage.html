Honorlock Instructions (Tests & Final)

Most courses that require online proctoring will use Honorlock. Honorlock is a remote proctoring system that uses advanced machine learning and facial detection technology to ensure test integrity. The program is optimized to protect your security and privacy while providing a proctored environment to replace classroom testing.

You'll take your exam on your own computer via the internet; exam sessions will be recorded and a live “pop-in” Honorlock proctor may interact with you if warranted. Recordings will be made available to your instructor, who can review them as needed.

To use Honorlock for proctored exams at UMSL, you need to use Google Chrome and install the Honorlock extension. You'll also need a webcam, microphone, and a quiet, well-lit space.

The Process: A Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Installation and Setup
    • Install Chrome: Ensure you're using the Google Chrome browser.
    • Install the Honorlock Extension: Add the Honorlock extension from the Chrome Web Store.
    • Confirm Browser Settings: You might need to adjust some browser security settings and restart Chrome.
    • System Check: Run the check for webcam, microphone, and internet connection.
  2. Exam Launch and Proctoring
    • Navigate to the Exam: In Canvas, open the Honorlock-enabled exam.
    • Launch Proctoring: Click “Launch Proctoring.”
    • Photo and ID Verification: Take a photo of yourself and your ID.
    • Room Scan: Use your webcam to scan your environment.
    • Start the Exam: Begin once the proctoring steps are complete.
  3. Inserting the Password
    • When the exam asks for a password, click the Honorlock button that says “I am ready to take the exam” or “Insert Password.”
    • Honorlock will auto-populate the password; click “Submit.”
  4. During the Exam
    • AI + Live Proctors: Monitoring runs throughout the exam.
    • Flags: Unusual behavior may be flagged and reviewed.
    • Support: 24/7 chat/phone/email via Honorlock.

Important Tips & Notes

  • Be in a well-lit space with a clear desk/walls.
  • No Honorlock account or appointment is required.
  • Use Chrome with the Honorlock extension; have a webcam, speakers, and mic.
  • Have a valid physical ID (UMSL ID, driver’s license, or passport) ready.
  • If issues arise, contact Honorlock Support (live chat or 844-243-2500).
