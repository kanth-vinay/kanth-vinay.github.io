About the Institute

The Active Learning Institute was founded in Warangal, India, with the support of the Center for Science and Mathematics Education at The University of Southern Mississippi. Its mission is to provide free mathematics education through active learning techniques, inspired by U.S. teaching methodologies.

Active Learning in Action

Witness the transformative power of active learning. Our initiatives empower students with essential skills and inspire a lifelong love of mathematics.

**Igniting Creativity:** Students dive into 3D modeling with TinkerCad, guided by Sharanya Narra. This hands-on workshop demonstrates how we make complex concepts like GCode accessible and engaging, fostering digital literacy and problem-solving skills. Your support helps us provide these crucial technological resources to aspiring innovators.

**Building Future Innovators:** Vinay Kodipelly leads a workshop where high school students explore the practical applications of mathematics in 3D printing. With equipment like our 4 Ender-3 3D printers, purchased through generous support, we equip students with the tools and knowledge to turn mathematical concepts into tangible creations, preparing them for careers in technology and engineering.

**Unlocking Digital Worlds:** Students discover the magic of computer graphics, guided by Vinay Kodipelly. By connecting fundamental mathematics to visual applications, using tools like our Alienware Area 51m laptop and projector, we inspire students to see the relevance of their studies in exciting, real-world contexts. Your donations make these immersive learning experiences possible.

**Empowering Communities:** Sharanya Narra, Education Administrator, shares the power of Vedic Mathematics with children at a local school in rural India. This outreach exemplifies our commitment to making quality mathematics education accessible to all, regardless of their background. Your support allows us to extend our reach and uplift communities through the power of education. This work was also featured in Voice of America.
