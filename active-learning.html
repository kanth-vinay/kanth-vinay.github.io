About the Institute

The Active Learning Institute was founded in Warangal, India, with the support of the Center for Science and Mathematics Education at The University of Southern Mississippi. Its mission is to provide free mathematics education through active learning techniques, inspired by U.S. teaching methodologies.

Active Learning in Action

Witness the transformative power of active learning. Our initiatives empower students with essential skills and inspire a lifelong love of mathematics.

Igniting Creativity: Students dive into 3D modeling with TinkerCad, guided by Sharanya Narra. This hands-on workshop demonstrates how we make complex concepts like GCode accessible and engaging, fostering digital literacy and problem-solving skills.

Building Future Innovators: Vinay Kodipelly leads a workshop where high school students explore the practical applications of mathematics in 3D printing. With equipment like our 4 Ender-3 3D printers, we prepare students for careers in technology and engineering.

Unlocking Digital Worlds: Students discover the magic of computer graphics with Vinay Kodipelly, connecting fundamental math to real-world applications.

Empowering Communities: Sharanya Narra shares the power of Vedic Mathematics with children, extending quality education to all.
