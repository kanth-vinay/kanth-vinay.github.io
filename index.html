Our Story & Approach

Active Learning Institute LLC, founded by Vinay Kanth Rao Kodipelly, is based in Saint Louis, Missouri and builds upon an educational initiative originally founded in India. With over 14 years of teaching experience in Math, Statistics, and Data Analytics, we are passionate about redefining math education. Inspired by innovative U.S. and Indian teaching methodologies, our programs use active learning strategies and technology integration (including AI-powered techniques) to help students build confidence, deep problem-solving skills, and academic excellence.

We focus on high-quality tutoring, accelerated math enrichment, and fostering equitable, student-centered learning environments through dynamic instructional practices and community outreach. Our work continues a legacy supported by institutions like The University of Southern Mississippi's Center for Science and Mathematics Education.

How We Approach Instruction

At Active Learning Institute, we put genuine, in-person engagement at the center of our teaching philosophy—especially for foundational courses. We know that the deepest learning happens not on a screen, but when students collaborate, ask questions, and receive immediate feedback in a supportive, small-group environment.

That’s why the vast majority of our foundational and enrichment programs are offered in-person, with small batches and hands-on activities. We also provide ongoing support through dedicated WhatsApp groups for each cohort, ensuring students and families can reach out for help or share progress at any time.

For our advanced, high-intensity courses—like AP Calculus AB/BC—we offer a flexible blend of in-person and online instruction to serve ambitious students with busy schedules or specialized needs. Our online sessions are interactive, never passive, and designed for students who are already comfortable with independent learning.

As our name says, we believe in Active Learning: learning by doing, discussing, exploring, and creating together. This is not just a slogan, but the core value shaping every class we offer.

ALI India Chapter

Active Learning Institute’s journey began in 2018 in Warangal, India with the support of the Center for Science and Mathematics Education at the University of Southern Mississippi. Our India chapter continues to be a vital part of our global educational mission.

We pioneered workshops on non-traditional STEM careers such as Mathematics for Animation, 3D Printing, Learning Human Anatomy through 3D Geometry using VR Sets, and Mathematical Applications in Computer Graphics. Our programs went beyond traditional paths, showcasing alternatives to conventional medical and engineering fields.

The India chapter has featured guest speakers including Karthik Adepu, who worked on blockbuster films like Fast and Furious, Tom and Jerry, and Kalki 2898. We’ve also partnered with NIT Warangal, hiring graduate students as interns and training them to become effective educators using active learning methodologies.
