About Me

Hi, I’m Vinay! I’m a Mathematics, Statistics, and Data Analytics Educator with over 15+ years of teaching experience, specializing in making math interactive, engaging, and applicable to real-world problems. I specialize in leveraging EdTech tools and AI-driven strategies to create engaging and effective learning experiences. I also provide tutoring and academic coaching services in mathematics and statistics.

I am a firm believer in the traditional 'chalk-and-talk' magic of classroom mentorship; however, I also recognize that modern teaching is riddled with systemic friction. To address this, alongside my active teaching, I build lightweight, offline-first infrastructural tools. My passion lies in creating instructional materials and helping educators bring technology-driven strategies into their teaching. I work at the intersection of mathematics, AI, and education, empowering both students and teachers with modern, hands-on learning approaches.
