My Commitment to Community Engagement

I have been actively involved in STEM outreach programs, including: - Co-organizing the **Mississippi Science and Engineering Fairs (2017, 2018, 2019)** - Serving as a judge for the Colorado Math Olympiad and VEX Robotics competitions (both local & international) - Serving as the 83rd and 84th William Lowell Putnam local supervisor - Conducting multiple technology-integration workshops for math teachers - Currently serving as a MathCounts Handbook reviewer for 2025-2026
