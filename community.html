My Commitment to Community Engagement

Over the years, I have been actively involved in multiple **STEM outreach programs**, from co-organizing **Mississippi Science and Engineering Fairs (2017, 2018, 2019)** to **serving as a judge for the Colorado Math Olympiad** and **VEX Robotics competitions (both local and international)**. I have also served as the **83rd and 84th William Lowell Putnam local supervisor**, conducted **multiple technology-integration workshops for math teachers**, and currently contribute as a **MathCounts Handbook reviewer for 2025-2026**.

Community Events

ACT Math Kahoot Challenge

Had an amazing time hosting the **ACT Math Kahoot Challenge**! Students actively engaged with problem-solving and preparation, and our **three winners received gift cards** sponsored by the **Upakara Foundation**.

This initiative is part of my volunteer efforts to **mentor and guide students** in their academic journey.

K-5 Robotics Event (Led by Sharanya Narra)

On **February 1st, 2025**, the **Active Learning Institute** hosted a successful **Robotics Fun for K-5 Kids** event at the **St. Louis County Library**.

Led by **Sharanya Narra**, the session introduced young students to **robotics using Edison Robots & Sphero Mini**, blending fun and learning.

The enthusiasm was so high that students asked for an **extra three hours** to continue experimenting with robots!
