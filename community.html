My Commitment to Community Engagement

I am deeply committed to giving back to the community through STEM outreach, teacher training, and supporting student competitions. My efforts focus on making math and technology accessible and engaging for all.

Key Contributions:

  • Co-organized the Mississippi Science and Engineering Fairs (2017, 2018, 2019)
  • Served as a judge for the Colorado Math Olympiad & VEX Robotics competitions
  • Supervised the 83rd & 84th William Lowell Putnam Mathematical Competitions
  • Conducted technology-integration workshops for math teachers
  • Currently serving as a MathCounts Handbook reviewer for 2025-2026
