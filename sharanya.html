Mission & Philosophy

As Co-Founder and Director of the Active Learning Institute (ActiveSTEM), I lead the design of curricula that challenge students to go beyond rote memorization. Our mission is to provide an "Institute Experience"—a rigorous, university-style learning environment that is distinct from franchise tutoring centers.

I specialize in the critical Middle-to-High School Transition. This is the "danger zone" where many students lose confidence. Through one-on-one apprenticeship, I use tools like robotics and coding to rebuild that confidence, proving that complex math is simply a tool for building cool things.

Institute Standard Designing curriculum that meets university-prep standards.

Active Construction Learning by building (Robotics, Coding, 3D Design).
