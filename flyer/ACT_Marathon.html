Competition Rules & Guidelines

Objective

Team up to solve as many ACT-style questions as possible in 2 hours. Collaborate, rotate roles, compete!

Team Roles

  • One runner
  • Two active solvers
  • Runner picks from Fresh Bowl, solves, moves slip to Used Bowl
  • Rotate runner each question

Core Rules

  • One question at a time (max 2 slips)
  • Runner switch every question
  • Record answers by question number
  • Holding >2 slips = warning; repeat = possible disqualification
  • No phones; ACT-approved calculators only
  • Volunteers collect sheets periodically
  • Most correct answers wins

What to Bring

  • Pens/pencils
  • ACT-approved calculator
  • Enthusiasm & focus

Tips

  • Communicate clearly
  • Stick to rotation
  • Work efficiently
  • Record before sending runner
